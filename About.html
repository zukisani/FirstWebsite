Who are we

Intelligence Technologies is founded by Masixole Bodla and Zukisani Nyayeni on the 04th of March 2014 in East London, Eastern Cape in South Africa.

We a value added Information Technology company supplying and implementing hardware and software that are complimented by the provision of associated support services and solutions. We ensure that the risks associated with the deployment of new technologies are reduced while providing competitive total cost of IT ownership that provides a compelling return on investment. Trusted by our clients to find innovative solutions from hardware and software to basic configuration and advanced design implementation, and even financing Intelligence Technologies is one of South Africa's leading single-source providers of IT products and services. Using a broad range of skills and technologies, Intelligence Technologies delivers IT products and services that support business-critical infrastructures and processes to businesses and organisations of all sizes and across every industry. With 11 distribution points across the country and 500 brand name product lines, our sales, technical and logistics ensure your expectations are met promptly and efficiently. We are fully BBBEE accredited, offering our clients valuable partnership. Specialist Software, Services and Leasing divisions are vital to our success, as well as that of our clients. Dedicated, professional account managers deliver the First Technology promise: To meet your needs within specific time frames and budgets.
